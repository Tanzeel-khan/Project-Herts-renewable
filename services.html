A grid connect system can have any type of generation whether it be solar PV, wind or hydro. This then connects into your distribution board and generated power is first used within the property to reduce electricity consumption and any surplus is exported to the grid or can be store in batteries.

OFF-GRID SOLAR PV

An Off-grid system is not connected to an external electricity supply and is often in a remote location or is mobile. Small off-grid systems offer an alternative to bringing in mains electricity. To be successful, efficient use of the electricity generated is essential and the system must be sized to meet worst case conditions.

HOW IT WORKS

A wind generator and/or solar PV panels charge a battery and the battery supplies power to the loads as needed. All loads are run at the battery voltage (usually 12 or 24 VDC).

WHAT IT CAN OFFER YOU

Here are some examples of what you could power

  • Music systems
  • Fridges
  • Lighting
  • Tools
  • TVs
  • Satellites
  • Telecoms equipment
  • Electric fencing

Cleaning solar panels

We are often asked about cleaning solar panels as they can accumulate dirt over time which can reduce the efficiency of sunlight capture and ultimately reduce the amount of electricity generated by your system. In most cases, the self-cleaning properties of the panels and the angle of installation is sufficient enough for the regular rainfall in the UK to remove any build up of dirt. Our experience is that this tends to make a solar panel clean uneconomical for the cost.
